A Legacy of Empowerment

"Magic Bus is one of the top five NGOs in the education and skilling space in India. Founded inpx Mumbai in 1999, we have grown into an organisation with a pan-India presence. Magic Bus works with young people living in poverty, taking them from a childhood full of challenges to a life with meaningful livelihoods. We work with young people’s ecosystems (parents, peers, community, and local institutions) sto create a network of support for them."

"Through our programmes focused on life skills education, employability skilling and education enhancement, Magic Bus ensures;"

  • "Adolescents complete secondary education"
  • "Youth are equipped for gainful employment"
